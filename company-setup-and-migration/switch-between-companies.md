# Switch Between Companies

Change the active company from the Companies page so the rest of the app updates to the company you want to work in.

## Purpose

Use this workflow when you need to move from one SPRK company to another without signing out.

## Prerequisites

- You can open `Settings` → `Companies`.
- More than one company is available in your workspace.

## Steps

1. Open `Settings` → `Companies`.
2. Find the company you want to work in.
3. Select `Select` in that company’s row.
4. Confirm that the button changes to `Selected`.
5. Move to any working page in the app and verify you are now operating in the intended company.

## Expected Result

The selected company becomes the active company across the app.

## Common Mistakes

- Assuming opening the row is enough. You need to use the `Select` action to make a company active.
- Forgetting to verify the active company before creating transactions or editing records.
- Switching companies during setup work and then continuing in the wrong company by accident.
