# Version 1.0 Capability Matrix

Use this public boundary to describe SPRK 1.0 without relying on internal coverage percentages.

## Positioning

SPRK 1.0 is a desktop accounting platform for accounting and bookkeeping firms serving small businesses. It covers core firm workflows: company setup, chart of accounts, banking, rules, reconciliation, invoices, bills, checks, journals, financial reports, backups, Company File handoff, support docs, and controlled plugin extensions.

## Included Workflow Families

- Company and client setup, including blank setup, guided import, company switching, and company-level maintenance.
- Chart of accounts and ledger review, including manual journals, journal import, reversals, auto-reversing entries, and audit-sensitive edit policies.
- Banking, including bank/card import, duplicate review, vendor review where available, rules, splits, matches, transfers, pending review, confirmation, and reconciliation.
- Sales and receivables, including customers, items, invoices, invoice payments, bank payment matching, payment history, linked journals, and unpaid-invoice voiding where visible and eligible.
- Expenses and payables, including vendors, bills, bill payments, checks, bank matching, payment history, linked journals, and posted-document correction policies.
- Reports, including trial balance, income statement, balance sheet, cash flow, general ledger, account detail, aging reports, expense by vendor, reconciliation reporting, export, print, and PDF-style review outputs where visible.
- Backups and Company File handoff, including local backup settings, on-demand backup, backup-on-close settings, and company-level export/import controls.
- Support documentation, release notes, support-log collection, and troubleshooting details.
- Plugin lifecycle basics, including bundle preview, install/upgrade, enable/disable, company availability, uninstall guardrails, and public runtime pages for supported plugin types.

## Explicit Exclusions

- Enterprise ERP replacement, consolidations, high-volume multi-entity close, and advanced role/security administration.
- Full multi-currency accounting, FX remeasurement, or translation workflows.
- Native payroll tax filing, tax agency filing, sales tax return filing, and jurisdiction-specific compliance filing.
- Advanced inventory costing, assemblies, landed cost, warehouse controls, or payroll compliance.
- Perfect historical QuickBooks migration of every transaction type.
- Fully automated accounting with no review.
- Hosted multi-user firm administration or cloud collaboration.
- Public plugin accounting schedules unless a visible runtime page is intentionally included and documented.

## Optimized 1.0 Focus

The most polished 1.0 workflows are banking and reconciliation, core AR/AP documents and payments, journal entry/reversal/import workflows, financial review and export, Company File handoff and backups, import-template discovery, support docs, and plugin lifecycle basics. Other workflow areas can still be useful without being marketed as equally optimized.
